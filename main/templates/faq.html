Help / FAQ

Questions

  1. I'm having trouble viewing content on the site!
  2. How do you I change my password or other personal info?
  3. How do I watch the videos sped up or slowed down?
  4. I've got ideas for more items to add to this page!

Answers

  1. If you're having any difficulties viewing content on the site, try using Chrome or Firefox as your browser.
  2. If you want to change your password, or any of your profile information, click on your name in the top navigation bar.
  3. If you disable Flash in your browser, you should get the HTML5 YouTube player when you view a video, and if you click on "Settings" (the cog near the bottom right of your video player), you'll find options to change the speed.
  4. Send suggestions for useful help items to c2g-suggestions@class.stanford.edu
