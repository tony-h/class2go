We are given the following corpus, similar to the one in lecture but with "ham" replaced by "Sam" and "I am Sam" included twice:

  • <s> I am Sam </s>
  • <s> Sam I am </s>
  • <s> I am Sam </s>
  • <s> I do not like green eggs and Sam </s>

Using a bigram language model with add-one smoothing, what is P(Sam | am)? Include <s> and </s> in your counts just like any other token.
\frac{3}{14}
  • \frac{2}{3}
  • \frac{3}{28}
  • \frac{2}{14}

Here V denotes the size of the vocabulary, which is the number if types in the corpus, not the number of tokens.

We add one to all counts, even those which are already non-zero.

The maximum-likelihood estimate is \frac{2}{3}

Using a bigram language model with add-one smoothing, P(Sam | am) = \frac{c(am, Sam) + 1}{c(am) + V} = \frac{2 + 1}{3 + 11} = \frac{3}{14}.
